The Stormie Moves Philosophy

Move together.

Explore often.

Stay curious.

New sports, new challenges, staying in motion, that's genuinely how I live, and I bring people along for it.

— Storm Kolmodin, Founder of Stormie Moves

I've always believed the best friendships start by doing something together, not by talking about it. That's why Stormie Moves exists. Climbing, trail walks, Muay Thai pad work, recovery sessions, it's never really about the exercise. It's about finding people who'd rather move through life than sit still.

No program, no pressure. Just movement, new places, and good company.
