Stormie Moves

Participation Terms

These terms apply to all Stormie Moves activities and community participation.

Participation

Stormie Moves is a community for recreational activities and shared experiences. Participation is voluntary and at your own risk.

Health

Participants are responsible for ensuring they are physically able to take part in an activity.

External Venues

Some activities take place at third-party venues. Participants must follow the rules, instructions and safety requirements of the venue at all times.

Equipment

Participants are responsible for bringing any required equipment unless otherwise stated.

Costs

Unless stated otherwise, participants are responsible for their own entrance fees, ferry tickets, equipment rentals and personal expenses.

Weather & Changes

Activities may be postponed, cancelled or changed due to weather or other circumstances.
